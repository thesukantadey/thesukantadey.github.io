Biography

I am a Graduate Student at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati), currently pursuing Doctorate of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). I joined IIT Guwahati in July 2014 and enrolled for the dual (M.Tech. + Ph.D) degree course in the dept. of CSE. My specialization is in Machine Learning techniques for Electronics Design Automation (EDA). My Ph.D. thesis title is Design Methodology for On-Chip Power Grid Interconnect: AI/ML Perspective. I will submit my Ph.D. thesis by September 2020 and will be available for joining in your esteemed institution from October 2020 onwards. My domain of expertise is Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Electronics Design Automation, VLSI Physical Design, Hardware Security, and Combinatorial Optimization. I have 6 years of experience in CAD tools development. I have excellent command in C, C++, Python programming languages with excellent debugging and troubleshooting skills. My works related to Ph. D. thesis have been published in reputed EDA journals/conferences, e.g., ACM TODAES journal, DATE, ISVLSI, and VLSID conferences. Before joining IIT Guwahati, I have completed my Bachelor's in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Assam Engineering College in 2014. I was born and raised in Guwahati, Assam, India.

About Me

I am a Graduate Student at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT Guwahati), currently pursuing Doctorate of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). I joined IIT Guwahati in July 2014 and enrolled for the dual (M.Tech. + Ph.D) degree course in the dept. of CSE. I have successfully completed Master of Technology (M.Tech.) coursework in December 2015 and Ph.D. comprehensive exam in March 2016. I have started my Ph.D. research work under the supervision of Prof. Sukumar Nandi, dept. of CSE and Dr. Gaurav Trivedi, dept. of EEE, IIT Guwahati. My research focuses on Analysis, Optimization and Verification of VLSI Power Distribution Network. Prior to joining IIT Guwahati, I have completed B.E. in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering in July 2014 from Assam Engineering College, Guwahati. I was born and raised in Guwahati, Assam, India.

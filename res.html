Research interests

VLSI CAD/Electronics Design Automation (EDA) and Desgin for Manufacturability(DFM)/ Reliability(DFR) for System-on-Chip (SoC).

The objective of this area is to develop CAD tools for solving various issues that occur in the SoC/chip design cycle. My primary focus is on developing various CAD tools for VLSI physical design flow. I have worked on several projects related to physical design CAD tool development. That includes stochastic DC and transient analysis tool, IR drop estimation tool, Electromigration-aware aging estimation tool, secure physical design & verification tool.

More-than-Moore interconnects for Multicore processor/ Multiprocessor SoC (MPSoC) - 3D IC, Network-on-Chip, Optical, Wireless.

The objective of this area is to develop CAD tools for design, analysis, and optimization of More-than-Moore interconnects. That includes 3D IC, NoC, Chiplets, and photonic IC. So far, I have been able to work on TSV-based 3D IC tool development. In future, I have plan to explore the other areas.

Machine Learning, Combinatorial Optimization, and Computational Intelligence in VLSI CAD.

The objective of this part is designing framework for predictive modeling and estimation using Machine Learning in VLSI CAD. Most of the VLSI CAD problems can be transformed as combinatorial optimization problem. Therefore in this part our another objective is to formulate VLSI CAD problems as combinatorial optimization problem and solve it with the help of Computation Intelligence or Nature Inspired Algorithms. For machine learning approaches, we are also considering data-driven approaches. I have also worked on few ML related VLSI CAD problems.

Hardware and Systems Security, Adversarial Machine Learning, Cryptography, Internet-of-Things (IoT).

In this part, the objective is to design secure system. Vulnerabilties may arise from various levels of abstraction. However, we should design the system in such a way in order to mitigate the vulnerabilties as less as possible. In that direction, we are addressing security and privacy issues related to hardware design flow from the circuit level to system level to application level. We are also conisdering secuirty issues in machine learning system., and secuirty $ design challenges in Internet-of-Things (IoT) systems.
